Privacy Policy for Strum&Sing

Effective date: April 19, 2026

Strum&Sing uses your device microphone to support tuner and vocal pitch features.

What data we use

Strum&Sing may access microphone audio only to analyze pitch and tuning in real time.

How data is used

Microphone audio is used to power tuning, pitch detection, and singing practice features.

Data storage

Strum&Sing does not sell your data. Microphone audio is intended to be processed on-device for app functionality.

Third parties

Strum&Sing does not share microphone audio with third parties for advertising or tracking.

Your choices

You can disable microphone access at any time in iPhone Settings.
