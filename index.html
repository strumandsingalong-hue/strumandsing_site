Strum Studio

Lock in rhythm before you add more complexity.

Start with a clean pattern, choose the practice sound you want, and follow the beat before adding more chords.

  • Metronome speed with count, click, and strum sound controls.
  • Simple pattern choices so beginners are not overloaded.
  • Stroke guidance that shows hits and air motion clearly.

Pitch

Find the scale your voice can actually use.

The Pitch tab helps you save a comfortable home note and notation style before moving into exercises.

  • Quick Detect for a fast starting point.
  • Guided flow for checking octave comfort.
  • Manual key, octave, and notation settings when you already know your scale.

Mystery Sequence

Make ear training feel like a small daily puzzle.

Mystery Sequence plays a short melody and asks you to sing it back. The app listens, locks notes as you hit them, and gives a hint without turning the exercise into a lecture.

  • Six daily sequences so practice stays short and achievable.
  • Difficulty changes the note choices, octave range, and sequence length.
  • Hints explain notes relative to the saved home note.
  • Music tips unlock after solved sequences before moving to the next one.

Toolkit

Practice with a steady home pitch when your ear is ready.

The Toolkit area includes the tanpura drone and Live Scale, helping you find your notes against a steady home-pitch reference.

  • Uses the scale saved in Pitch.
  • Starter guidance and practice support live beside the drone.
  • Live scale graph shows your voice moving through the notes.
